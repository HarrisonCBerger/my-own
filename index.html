Monkey D Luffy

About Luffy

Luffy is a pirate who wants to be the king of the pirate. He, along with his crew, are in search for the One Piece!

Luffy and his crew

Luffy's Characteristics

  1. When he was a kid, he ate the Gum Gum devil fruit, giving him the power to be a man made of rubber.
  2. Luffy is always hungry, and he eats a lot. His favorite food is meat.
  3. Luffy is very good at getting people to be his friend. He sometimes is a little quick in asking someone to join his crew.

Luffy's Friends

    Luffy has a lot of strong friends. They are all after their own specific goals, but by being together, they know they can achieve them.

  • Roronoa Zoro
